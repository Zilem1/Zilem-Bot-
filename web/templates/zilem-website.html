Questions

FAQ

What does Zilem Method actually do?
It patches the MP4 box structure (moov/mdat) directly — adjusting sample tables, chunk offsets, and fake sample entries — without touching your video or audio data. No re-encoding means no quality loss and near-instant processing.
Does my file get uploaded anywhere?
Never. Everything runs in your browser. Your file is read into browser memory, patched there, and downloaded back to you. No server ever sees your file.
How do I get a key?
Join our Discord server and use the bot command to generate your key. The key encodes your tier level and is verified client-side. Keys expire after 30 days or when you request a new one.
How do file size limits work?
You need a key from our Discord bot to use the patcher. Your file size limit is set by your Discord role — Member gets 150MB, Booster 750MB, and Donor 1GB.
Will the patched file look different?
No. The video and audio streams are byte-for-byte identical to the original. Only the container metadata is modified.
What formats are supported?
The patcher works on MP4, MOV, and any MP4-container format (M4V, M4A). MKV, AVI, and WebM use different container formats and are not currently supported.
